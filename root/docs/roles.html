Role-based Authorization The role-based authorization built into the app lets us easily provide different views based on the status of the user. Here's how it works. Available Roles: * admin App developers. Have full privileges plus dashboard panels for debugging and performance tuning. * manager A senior user, such as a principal investigator. Managers have access to reporting and statistics. * employee A semi-privileged member of the business or lab. Employees can enter and edit data. * users End users. Can create an account for themself and place order. Where are roles defined? * schema/cgc.sql How are the ancestral admin users specified? * During database creation in schema/cgc.sql * Dynamically based on specific email addresses in App::Web::Controller::Auth. How do I make use of roles in templates to conditionally display content? [% IF c.check_user_role('admin') %] You're awesome, admin! Have an awesome day, m'kay? [% END %] What else? Checkout Catalyst::Plugin::Authorization::Role for additional cool stuff. For example, we may want to restrict by role access to specific actions.
